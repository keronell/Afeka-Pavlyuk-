Place 3 – Cafeteria of the Kiriya

The Cafeteria of the Kiriya is one of the most iconic structures at Afeka collage. Its unique architecture and vibrant surroundings make it a favorite spot for students and faculty alike.

Short subtitle about this place

My first lecture at Afeka was held in the Cafeteria of the Kiriya. I remember being amazed by the modern design and the welcoming atmosphere. The building houses several important departments and offers stunning views of the campus.

Besides its academic significance, the Cafeteria of the Kiriya is also known for its beautiful landscaping. The surrounding gardens and seating areas provide a perfect place for relaxation and socializing between classes.
